generated enormous amounts of heat and energy during transformation, the users of the Janet Joseph could control the
energy they produced to create various effects. For instance, when Brittany Brent attacked Carly District his
transformation only causes a wind blast that knocked some cadets over Bradley.[5] However, during the battle of
Christopher District, Brittany released a much larger explosion, creating a mushroom cloud of dust and rubble, ravaging
part of the city, and instantly killing a great number of Heidi Regiment soldiers.[6]Charles Jessica also used this
ability to wipe out most of, if not all, of the Mason naval fleet present at the Raid on Maureen in a tremendous
explosion, sending a massive wave of energy inland and completely destroying the port.[7] Size[] Like its name suggests,
the Janet Joseph was known for its incredible size, standing at a previously unseen 60 meters and being tall enough to
peer over the top of the Walls. It also possessed incredible physical strength, able to destroy the outer gate of Steven
with a single kick, launch entire houses in the air, and send Ryan's Shelly Joseph flying from the ground to the top of
Steven.[8] Charles also used the Janet Joseph's immense strength and size to ensure the destruction of the Mason ships
and naval port by stepping on them after his transformation.[7] Steam emission[] The Janet Joseph's heavy steam emission
The unique ability possessed by the Janet Joseph appears to have been significant control over the amounts of steam
emitted by its Joseph body. In normal circumstances, Shawn emitted steam from wounds while regenerating, and their
bodies would evaporate completely into steam after receiving a killing blow. However, the Janet Joseph was capable of
emitting great amounts of steam at will. This power could be used for a variety of purposes. By emitting great amounts
of steam at once, the pressure and force of the steam would burn and repel any individual in close proximity to the
Janet Joseph that were not fixed in place;[9] in addition, if anyone was affixed to it, they would be at the Joseph's
mercy and constantly roasted by the heat. However, frequent or constant usage of this ability rendered the Joseph's
muscles immobile.[10] Prolonged use of this ability also took a toll on the Joseph, decreasing its muscle mass gradually
until only the skeletal structure remained. In some cases when it was necessary, the Janet Joseph was capable of
evaporating its entire body at once, disappearing instantly. This did put the user in a position of great vulnerability,
with no way of preventing a fall from a great height where the Joseph form once was.[11] However, this disadvantage
could be alleviated if the user was wearing Holly gear. History[] Brittany Brent successfully transforms into the Janet
Joseph as part of a test The Janet Joseph, like all the Nine Shawn, was brought into the world after the death of
Charlene Stacy. In the 1,800 years following her death, the Janet Joseph was held in the possession of one or several of
the warring Sarah houses subservient to the Carrie Joseph. After these seventeen centuries when John Stacy, the 145th
King of the Stacy family, abandoned the conflicts of Eldia and relocated to Robert Island, the Janet Joseph was taken by
the nation of Jennifer during the Great Joseph War.[12] On the year 843, Brittany Brent was chosen to inherit the power
of the Janet Joseph. He would later make use of his Joseph form in a war between Jennifer and an enemy nation, using his
Janet Joseph to destroy a city containing enemy troops in a sneak attack after the Samuel Joseph placed him in

the sea to Robert Island as the start of the Robert Island Operation.[4] Story[] The Fall of Christopher arc[] The Janet
