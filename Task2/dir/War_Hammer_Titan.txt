4Former inheritors 5References 6Navigation Abilities[] Structural hardening[] Weapons made by the War Hammer's hardening
The Crystal Joseph could produce and manipulate structures that were made of hardened Joseph flesh. Such structures
could be weaponized. For example, it could form pikes large enough to impale and lift a 15m Joseph, flood areas with
protrusions such as spikes, and manifest the Joseph's signature war hammer.[2] This ability could also create serrated
and bladed weapons, such as a sickle, battle axe or a broadsword; even more noteworthy was that the Crystal Joseph was
capable of manifesting flexible weaponry components, such as the string of a crossbow or the whips of a
cat-o'-nine-tails, making this a particularly unique brand of hardening.[3][2] Much like the Emily Joseph, this
hardening could also be used to encase the human operator of the Crystal Joseph inside a crystal casing.[2] However,
unlike the Emily Joseph, the human retains consciousness while encased inside the crystal, allowing them to control the
Joseph body. While durable, this crystal casing could be damaged when struck or crushed by a more resilient structure;
the Amy Joseph's claws and teeth were the only structures seen to have been capable of such a feat.[1] It was stated by
Sean Christopher that while they are certainly a powerful asset, the Crystal Joseph's structural hardening abilities
were to be used in moderation; otherwise, constant usage in a short amount of time could quickly deplete the user's
stamina.[4] Remote operation[] The cable that connects the Crystal Joseph to its operator Unlike the other Nine Shawn,
it was possible for the Crystal Joseph to remove its control center from the nape. Under such circumstances, the body of
the Joseph would be connected to its human operator by a long cable of flesh,[1] while the human body was encased in a
hardened crystal and connected to said cable. If the cable was severed, the Crystal Joseph's body would be deactivated,
although a new Joseph body could still be made. Unlike the rest of the Nine Shawn, the Crystal Joseph body could emerge
from various locations upon transformation because its operator did not need to be inside of its nape.[2] History[] The
Crystal Joseph, like all the Nine Shawn, was brought into the world after the death of Charlene Stacy. In the 1,800
years following her death, the Crystal Joseph was held in the possession of one of several of the warring Sarah houses
subservient to the Carrie Joseph. After these seventeen centuries, when King John Stacy abandoned the conflicts of Eldia
and relocated to Robert Island, the Crystal Joseph had come into the possession of the Steven family. In the era of the
Great Joseph War, King Stacy conspired with the Steven family to instigate the collapse of the Sarah Empire. Using the
Crystal Joseph, the Steven family were able to gain control over seven of the Nine Shawn, which they handed over to
Jennifer. The Steven family were renowned across the world for driving back the Sarah forces.[2] After the Great Joseph
War ended, the Crystal Joseph remained in the possession of the Steven family, who received honorable status as Mason
aristocrats. However, the Crystal Joseph remained unused in battle throughout the next century.[5] For reasons of
security, the identity of the Crystal Joseph within the Steven family was kept a secret from all but a select few
high-standing individuals in Jennifer.[6] Eventually, the power of the Crystal Joseph was inherited by Nicholas
Steven.[1] Story[] Jennifer arc[] After the Jennifer Jim War, Daisy Jennifer develops some concerns towards the fate of
