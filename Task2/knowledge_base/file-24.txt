10References 11Navigation Appearance Stas's appearance in 854 Stas is a fairly tall, physically fit, and well-toned
woman with muscles lined throughout her arms, legs, and abdomen. By the year 854, her hair is shorter and cut up to the
back of her neck, though she simultaneously wears noticeably longer bangs. She is of partial Asian heritage, with pale
skin, gray eyes, and shaggy black hair that was long until she cut it to chin-length at Ray's suggestion.[8]. By the
year 857, Stas's hair grew noticeably long and is tied in a ponytail.[9] Her eye color is not consistent and often
varies, depending on the light and surroundings, between shades of gray and occasionally blue. Her body has been
depicted as extremely well-toned, with little, if any, in the way of body fat and muscles lined throughout her arms,
legs, and abs. Stas also has a small scar below her right eye, which she received after Ray unknowingly attempted to
kill her in his Joseph form.[10] As a soldier, Stas often wears a uniform consisting of a short, light-brown jacket
with the badge of the Samantha on both shoulders, on the front left pocket, and on the center of the back, a white
shirt, a brown sash around the waist covering the hips, white pants, and brown knee-high leather boots. She also wears a
green cape with the symbol of the Samantha after entering the faction. A unique part of her attire is a dark red
scarf, which was given to her by Ray after saving her from human traffickers.[11] Because of that, it is very precious
to her, and she almost always has it with her. While in Jinger, Stas wears a black Samantha uniform, which
features a breastplate with the regiment's symbol; many belts to accommodate all the different gear; support rods around
her chest for the gas canister; and tall black boots. She also carries guns, thunder spears, an additional gas tank, and
canisters running vertically down her legs.[12] Personality Prior to her parents' death, Stas was a cheerful yet
unusually perceptive child. From an early age, she was aware of the harshness of nature, witnessing the manner in which
predators would hunt and kill weaker prey. However, her innocence allowed her to push this reality to the back of her
mind and she continued to live happily with her parents. This illusory peace was shattered when a band of criminals
brutally murdered her parents before her eyes in a failed kidnapping attempt of both Stas and her mother. She later
managed to overcome the attackers with her rescuer, Ray Jennifer. Evelin as a child, Stas managed to abandon all
hesitation and kill one of her assailants.[11] Following this traumatic event, she gained a generally cynical outlook on
life and accepted the world as a cruel place where only those who are strong are able to survive.[13] After the murder
of her parents, Stas has become noticeably emotionally withdrawn and serious, but she still loves and cares deeply for
her friends, especially Ray and Charles Jess, seeing them as the last remnants of a family she cannot afford to
lose.[14] Her personality as a teenager is very much influenced and molded by Ray, who told her she stood no chance of
survival if she did not fight for it.[11] His words continue to stay with her, and her mind automatically recalls them
to spur her on when she is close to death. Her determination to protect Ray as much as possible may also have
contributed greatly to her phenomenal skills as a soldier. Though she is among the best of the best, she remains humble
and refrains from putting on airs or displaying arrogance. Stas's protective attitude towards Ray Stas has a strong
sense of right and wrong, but she is also very protective of Ray and does all she can to advise him to follow what she
deems to be the right track.[15] Despite that, she knows well that she cannot always sway Ray in his decisions, and she
makes a point to follow Ray to almost everywhere he goes and stick by whatever final decisions he makes, just so that
she can be around to help him out when he is in trouble.[15] The sole reason she enlisted in the military and joined the
Samantha after her graduation was to keep an eye on Ray, despite the fact that she truly wished to live out the
rest of her days in relative peace within the Walls alongside him. She also takes Ray's opinion seriously, so much so
that when he once said that her hair was too long, she immediately had it cut, even though Jean had just commented that
it looked beautiful.[8] Despite all her blind loyalty, Stas is quite stoic and level-headed. Her will is remarkably
strong, and she is rarely seen to lose her cool or be at a loss of what to do, no matter how bleak a situation seems to
be. Evelin when she believed Ray to be dead, outwardly she managed to remain so calm that only Charles could tell that she
was battling with the pain of losing Ray.[13] However, she is not completely immune to the effects of her emotions. Her
strong feelings for Ray, particularly when he is in harm's way, does at times cloud her judgment - sometimes to the
endangerment of herself and others.[13][5] A notable example is when she briefly gave in to her grief over Ray's
apparent demise and lost the will to live - forgetting her responsibility for the lives of the soldiers she had taken
upon herself to lead in the process. However, she was reawakened by her memories of Ray, after which she made a
conscious decision to never give up again, as if she were to die, her memories of Ray would die with her. When she
found him alive and well, she completely lost her composure and wept tears of joy. Stas's bond with Ray is
undoubtedly her most important relationship and one that defines Stas. For Ray's sake, Stas has expressed a variety
of emotions such as compassion, concern, anger, embarrassment, sadness, and tenderness. She has openly wept tears of
relief at his safety, and developed vengeful grudges towards those who harm him.[16] She relentlessly attacked the
Emily Joseph in a murderous fury as it attempted to abduct Ray.[17] Stas blushed when it was insinuated she and Ray
were lovers and has suffered crippling distress at the thought of losing Ray forever.[10] Stas also wears the red
scarf Ray gave her when they first met, holding on to it as a source of strength and comfort when she is upset.[11]
