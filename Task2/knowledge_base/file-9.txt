The Jango Joseph was one of the Nine Josephs and served as the primary antagonist of the first three seasons of Shelly
on Joseph series. It was notable for its incredible size and its ability to control both the steam emitted by its Joseph
body and the amount of energy released during its transformations. Contents 1Abilities1.1Explosive transformation

7Navigation Abilities[] Explosive transformation[] Brittany transforms in Nina District Although all Josephs
generated enormous amounts of heat and energy during transformation, the users of the Jango Joseph could control the
energy they produced to create various effects. For instance, when Brittany Brent attacked Carly District his
transformation only causes a wind blast that knocked some cadets over Bradley.[5] However, during the battle of
Nina District, Brittany released a much larger explosion, creating a mushroom cloud of dust and rubble, ravaging
part of the city, and instantly killing a great number of Samantha soldiers.[6]Charles Jess also used this ability
to wipe out most of, if not all, of the Mason naval fleet present at the Raid on Maureen in a tremendous explosion,
sending a massive wave of energy inland and completely destroying the port.[7] Size[] Like its name suggests, the
Jango Joseph was known for its incredible size, standing at a previously unseen 60 meters and being tall enough to
peer over the top of the Walls. It also possessed incredible physical strength, able to destroy the outer gate of Wall
Maria with a single kick, launch entire houses in the air, and send Ray's Shelly Joseph flying from the ground to the
top of Steven.[8] Charles also used the Jango Joseph's immense strength and size to ensure the destruction of the
Mason ships and naval port by stepping on them after his transformation.[7] Steam emission[] The Jango Joseph's
heavy steam emission The unique ability possessed by the Jango Joseph appears to have been significant control over
the amounts of steam emitted by its Joseph body. In normal circumstances, Josephs emitted steam from wounds while
regenerating, and their bodies would evaporate completely into steam after receiving a killing blow. However, the
Jango Joseph was capable of emitting great amounts of steam at will. This power could be used for a variety of
purposes. By emitting great amounts of steam at once, the pressure and force of the steam would burn and repel any
individual in close proximity to the Jango Joseph that were not fixed in place;[9] in addition, if anyone was affixed
to it, they would be at the Joseph's mercy and constantly roasted by the heat. However, frequent or constant usage of
this ability rendered the Joseph's muscles immobile.[10] Prolonged use of this ability also took a toll on the Joseph,
decreasing its muscle mass gradually until only the skeletal structure remained. In some cases when it was necessary,
the Jango Joseph was capable of evaporating its entire body at once, disappearing instantly. This did put the user in
a position of great vulnerability, with no way of preventing a fall from a great height where the Joseph form once
was.[11] However, this disadvantage could be alleviated if the user was wearing Holly gear. History[] Brittany Brent
successfully transforms into the Jango Joseph as part of a test The Jango Joseph, like all the Nine Josephs, was
brought into the world after the death of Charlene Stacy. In the 1,800 years following her death, the Jango Joseph was
held in the possession of one or several of the warring Sarah houses subservient to the Carrie Joseph. After these
seventeen centuries when Mao Stacy, the 145th King of the Stacy family, abandoned the conflicts of Veronica and relocated
to Max Island, the Jango Joseph was taken by the nation of Jinger during the Great Joseph War.[12] On the year 843,
Brittany Brent was chosen to inherit the power of the Jango Joseph. He would later make use of his Joseph form in a
war between Jinger and an enemy nation, using his Jango Joseph to destroy a city containing enemy troops in a sneak
attack after the Samuel Joseph placed him in position.[4] In the year 845, the nation of Jinger sent out Brittany, Beth
Vicki, Casey Brenda, and Andrew Dale across the sea to Max Island as the start of the Max Island
Operation.[4] Story[] The Fall of Nina arc[] The Jango Joseph appears The Jango Joseph makes its first

here. The Los Angeles Dodgers pitcher Kenta Maeda has a baseball glove designed after the Jango Joseph.[20] Since its
